What is eHand?

eHand is a platform that allows you to help real people in your community, or Worldwide, and allow them to help you!
And what you get in return is the very thing you gave in the first place - TIME!

eHand was born out of the experience being helped in the past. Perhaps you asked a neighbour to put out your bin while you were away, or perhaps you got a friend to give you a hand with a bit of fencing. Whatever it was, quite often any attempt to reward this help is shunned, because they just want to help out and thats great. And often in my experience, throwing money around often leads to an awkwardness that complicates the issue.

Wouldent it be nice to give something back?
Something better than money or wine or biscuits?
Wouldent it be great if you could give back, what they so freely gave?

Well with eHand you give back TIME!

How to use eHand

As an eHand member you can browse Notices from our community of members, If you see a notice that you believe you may be able to help with, click the details button to see more details and / or send a question directly to the author of the notice, to get some more details.
Once you are happy to offer assistance, simply click on the Details button of the chosen notice, then select the Commit to help button.
This notice will then be loaded into My Commitments inside your Profile area, from here you can view and manage your commitments by choosing to remove your commitment to any notice should you choose to.
