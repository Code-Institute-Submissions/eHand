Make Time, by Giving Time..

eHand was born out of the experiences of helping and getting help from family and friends.
Be it:

  • Asking someone to keep an eye on things while your away
  • Looking after a family pet
  • Getting a local handyman to help with some fencing
  • Getting Maths lessons from a local teacher
  • etc...
Now quite often these sorts of favours go unpaid - as no payment is generally required, or sought, for an act of kindness.

But what if you could give something back to them, something that they gave freely to you? An hour, 2 hours perhaps?

Well this is what eHand does - it gives back the gift of time.

Get Started

As an eHand Premium member you can browse Notices from our community of members and also post your own. These notices will be visable to all members giving you a much wider net to seek the help you require.

When you sign up for a premium membership, you will receive 10 hours in your Time Account. These can be used to pay other members for help they give to you.

Earn more hours by helping other members, and also attending community clean up days, or other organized charity events - where members will receive a Time Bonus.

Get Started

It is so easy to get up and going with eHand:

  • Click on Get Started to either Sign in or Register for an account if you are a new user
  • Choose to upgrade to Premium in the upgrade section - or continue as a free member
  • View notices in the Notice section.
  • If you think you can help, click Details and commit to help - or ask a question in the comments section
  • Once committed to a Notice you are expected to fulfil your obligation - your commitments are displayed in your profile, under "My Commitments"
  • Please note that any member may commit to help anyone on eHand - but only Premium members hold a Time Account, enabling Time Transfers.
  • Once you have finished helping, the member may transfer the Time to you and choose to delete the Notice, from his noices section within his profile.
  • To create a Notice, simply click the add notice in the Notices section, Please Note - only Premium members can create a Notice.

Memberships may be cancelled at any time by the member. A Member who cancells their Premium membership will also cancel their Time account.

Please note, the proceeds of Monthly subscriptions are donated to Local charities and community schemes to further spread the helping eHand!!
